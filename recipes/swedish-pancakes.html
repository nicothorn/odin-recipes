Swedish Pancakes

Photo by Maria Borisenko via Pexels.

Description

This is my family's Swedish pancakes recipe that has been passed down from my paternal great-great+ grandmother. It's simple, yet delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 & 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • dash of salt

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except flour to blender.
  2. Blend for a few seconds until mixed.
  3. Slowly add flour to blender, mixing until it's incorporated, but ensuring not to mix for too long.
  4. Heat pan on medium heat and melt enough butter to lightly coat the pan.
  5. Add about a quarter cup of batter to the pan, and then tilt the pan in a circle so the batter spreads evenly.
  6. When the batter becomes opaque (not shiny) on top, use a spatula to gently lift the edges, and then flip the pancake.
  7. Cook for a short amount of time, until slightly browned, and then remove from the pan.
  8. Repeat with remaining batter, melting butter in the pan before each new pancake.
  9. Top with your favorite toppings! Some popular options are: maple syrup, powdered sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, lingonberries, or other berries!
